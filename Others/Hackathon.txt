SupplyChain Nexus: An Intelligent Supply Chain Risk Analytics and Decision Support
Platform

Problem Statement
Modern supply chains require balancing warehouse capacities, transportation costs, delivery times,
and regional demand while minimizing operational risks. Existing analytical tools primarily provide
descriptive insights through dashboards, requiring managers to manually interpret data and make
decisions. As supply networks grow in complexity, identifying bottlenecks and determining the
most ef cient distribution strategy becomes increasingly challenging.

Abstract
SupplyChain Nexus is an intelligent Supply Chain Risk Analytics and Decision Support Platform
that assists organizations in analyzing warehouse utilization, identifying logistics bottlenecks, and
optimizing distribution strategies using operational supply chain data. The platform integrates
warehouse capacities, regional demand, transportation costs, travel times, distance matrices, and
geographical information to provide a comprehensive understanding of the supply chain network.
The system computes warehouse utilization, evaluates operational risk based on capacity and
demand distribution, and identi es inef cient logistics patterns. A multi-objective optimization
engine recommends improved warehouse allocations by minimizing transportation cost and
delivery time while balancing warehouse utilization across the network.
To support strategic planning, the platform incorporates a Scenario Analysis Engine that allows
decision-makers to evaluate the effects of warehouse outages, capacity reductions, and demand
uctuations. For each simulated scenario, the system recalculates warehouse assignments, logistics
costs, delivery times, and utilization levels, enabling organizations to proactively assess risks and
compare alternative distribution strategies.
The platform presents these insights through an interactive geospatial dashboard featuring
warehouse locations, optimized transportation routes, utilization heatmaps, operational alerts, and
AI-generated recommendations. By combining analytics, optimization, simulation, and
visualization into a uni ed platform, SupplyChain Nexus enables organizations to make faster, datadriven decisions that improve operational ef ciency, reduce logistics costs, and strengthen supply
chain resilience.

recommends optimized warehouse allocations and allows decision-makers to evaluate alternative
logistics strategies before implementation.
