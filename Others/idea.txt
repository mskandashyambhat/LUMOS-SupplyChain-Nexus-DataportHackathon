fi

SupplyChain Nexus acts as an AI-assisted supply chain decision support platform. It transforms
raw logistics data into optimized warehouse allocations, scenario-based planning, explainable
recommendations, and interactive visualizations, enabling organizations to make faster, data-driven
decisions that reduce costs, improve ef ciency, and increase supply chain resilience.
